Current Positioning

How BeatBright should function inside Blackshield Institute

BeatBright should not be treated as a random isolated tool with no business logic. It works best as a premium interactive feature inside the larger Blackshield system.

That means BeatBright should first strengthen memberships and flagship access before being considered as a standalone product.

Recommended Pricing Structure

Clean product logic for BeatBright deployment

Included in Diamond

BeatBright should be included in Diamond in the Rough as part of premium full-system access.

Included in Tier II

Tier II should include BeatBright as a higher-value interactive tool within recurring membership.

Preview in Tier I

Tier I can preview BeatBright or link to limited access, but should not carry full premium positioning.

Standalone Product Decision

Should BeatBright be sold alone?

Recommended Answer: Not Yet

Right now BeatBright is more valuable as a membership enhancer than as a standalone product. Selling it alone too early weakens your tier structure.

Future Standalone Option

Later, BeatBright can become a separate offer with a one-time price or monthly access, but only after the memberships and flagship path are fully stabilized.
