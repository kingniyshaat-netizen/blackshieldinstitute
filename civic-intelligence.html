Program Overview

Thinking clearly about community, structure, and responsibility

Civic Intelligence is the ability to understand how communities function, how institutions shape behavior, and how individuals contribute to social order.

This curriculum is built to develop awareness, responsibility, and disciplined participation in family, neighborhood, and broader civic environments.

Core Development Areas

Institutional Awareness

Understanding how systems and organizations affect daily life.

Responsibility

Recognizing personal obligations within a functioning community.

Disciplined Participation

Learning how to contribute constructively rather than passively.
