# Blackshield Institute

Blackshield Institute is a digital curriculum, publishing, and learning platform built for structured education, literacy, family restoration, youth development, leadership, discipline, and practical life systems.

The platform includes books, curriculum tracks, membership tiers, interactive tools, product pages, and digital learning pathways.

## Curriculum Areas

Blackshield Institute includes curriculum pages for literacy, family structure, digital citizenship, financial literacy, civic intelligence, leadership development, technology systems, ethical reasoning, conflict resolution, entrepreneurship, community building, cultural heritage, and more.

## Product Status Labels

Every product should be labeled as one of the following:

- **Available**
- **Preview**
- **Early Access**
- **Coming Soon**
- **Prototype**

## Repository Rules

1. Every public product page must have a clear title, description, audience, status, and action button.
2. Every product button must say exactly what it does: Open, Preview, Buy, Join Early Access, Download, or Contact.
3. Downloads should use clean file names such as `product-name-preview-v1.pdf`.
4. Admin pages are public unless real authentication is added.
5. Unfinished products belong in `staging/`.
6. Legal pages must be reviewed before collecting payments, emails, or learner data.
7. New curriculum pages should be linked from `curriculum.html` or `catalog.html`.
