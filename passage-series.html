PASSAGES

Foundational Cultural Formation for Youth

Modern youth are exposed to information, but lack structured formation in safety, boundaries, emotional discipline, and personal responsibility.

PASSAGES restores disciplined formation through measurable execution.

What This Curriculum Builds

  • Emergency readiness and self-identification
  • Boundary enforcement and refusal scripts
  • Identity clarity and structured thinking
  • Emotional discipline under pressure
  • Responsible habit formation
  • Health and maintenance literacy

Recommended Age Range

Ages 6–12

Parent-guided for ages 6–8. Independent-capable for ages 9–12.

Implementation Structure

  • 5 Modules
  • 1–2 sessions per week
  • 20–30 minutes per session
  • Estimated completion: 5–6 weeks

After Completion, Your Child Will:

  • Recite emergency contact information confidently
  • Demonstrate refusal and boundary scripts
  • Identify trusted adults and safety protocols
  • Understand basic consent mechanics
  • Maintain structured hygiene routines

Certification

PASSAGES includes repetition tracking, recall verification, 7-day retention checks, and a Certificate of Completion issued upon verified completion.
