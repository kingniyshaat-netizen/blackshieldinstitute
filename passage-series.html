PASSAGES Series

PASSAGES is a structured children’s series built to teach essential developmental knowledge, safety awareness, boundaries, identity, decision-making, and practical growth. Each title is designed to be direct, memorable, and developmentally useful.

What the Series Covers

The PASSAGES line teaches children and young readers practical concepts such as names, phone numbers, address recall, trusted adults, what to do when lost, healthy growth, boundaries, and age-appropriate development.

This series is positioned as a premium institutional children’s collection under Blackshield Institute.
