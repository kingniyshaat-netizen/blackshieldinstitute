The Problem

Modern youth receive information without formation. PASSAGES restores disciplined behavioral literacy through measurable execution across safety, boundaries, identity, emotional discipline, and maintenance habits.

After Completion, Youth Will Demonstrate

Safety & Readiness

  • Recite emergency information without hesitation
  • Execute “lost” protocol correctly
  • Identify trusted authority figures

Boundaries & Identity Discipline

  • Demonstrate refusal and boundary scripts
  • Define identity by conduct, not labels
  • Apply anti-teasing clarity scripts

Responsibility & Emotional Control

  • Maintain routine discipline (hygiene, chores)
  • Name emotions without chaos
  • Practice service-minded responsibility

Consent & Health Maintenance

  • Understand consent mechanics at a foundational level
  • Recognize body signals and healthy supports
  • Build weekly maintenance routines (sleep, hydration)
